A **Baggage Train** is a set of five cards used to outline the abilities of a player's Wagon. These were included with the English 4th and 5th editions (2nd and 3rd international editions) of [_Traders & Barbarians_][traders-barbarians], but were replaced in the English 6th edition (4th international edition) with the [Wagon Board][wagon-board].

## Appearance
Each player has a single Baggage Train, consisting of a series of five cards arranged in a specific order. Each card has a background matching one of the player colors, and are otherwise identical between players. Each card is associated with a level, which contains information on the Wagon's movement range, ability to drive off Barbarians, and the gold received when [Commodity Tokens][commodity-token] are delivered.

## Gameplay
- At the start of the game, the five cards are stacked face-down in order by level, with the Level 1 card at the top. Before the game begins, the top card is flipped face-up and placed next to the face-down cards.
- A player may pay the upgrade cost of the wagon, depicted on the back of the next card in the face-down stack, to flip the card face-up. This card is then placed on top of any previously flipped cards.
- **Full Upgrade Bonus**: A player having flipped all five cards face-up earns one additional victory point.

| Level | Movement | Delivery | Drive Off | Upgrade Cost            |
|-------|----------|----------|-----------|-------------------------|
| 1     | 4        | 1 gold   |           |                         |
| 2     | 5        | 2 gold   | 6         | 1 lumber, 1 ore, 1 wool |
| 3     | 6        | 3 gold   | 5-6       | 1 lumber, 1 ore, 1 wool |
| 4     | 7        | 4 gold   | 4-6       | 2 lumber, 1 ore, 1 wool |
| 5     | 7        | 5 gold   | 3-6       | 2 lumber, 1 ore, 1 wool |

## Manifest
1 set of 5 cards for the red, blue, orange, and white players are included with the English 4th and 5th editions (2nd and 3rd international editions) of [_Traders & Barbarians_][traders-barbarians]. 2 additional sets, for the green and brown players, are included with the English 4th and 5th editions (2nd and 3rd international editions) of the [_Traders & Barbarians_][traders-barbarians] extension.
