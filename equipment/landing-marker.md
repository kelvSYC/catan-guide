**Landing Markers** are a type of marker placed on hexes in the scenarios of [_Legend of the Conquerors_][conquerors].

## Appearance
Landing markers are identical in appearance between English and international sets. They are irregularly-shaped pieces depicting a [conqueror][conqueror] holding a flag.

## Gameplay
Landing markers are placed on the board in the locations where [conquerors][conqueror] enter the board. They are placed on the board during setup, and remain in their locations for the entirety of the game.

## Manifest
4 landing markers are included with [_Legend of the Conquerors_][conquerors].
