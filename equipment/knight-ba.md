**Knights** are pieces introduced in [_Traders & Barbarians_][traders-barbarians], for use with the scenario "Barbarian Attack".

Note that design-wise, the knight overlaps with the concept of [its counterpart][knight-ck] in [_Cities & Knights_][cities-knights]; indeed, knights are only used when playing "Barbarian Attack" without [_Cities & Knights_][cities-knights]. The main difference between the two different versions is that the knights of [_Traders & Barbarians_][traders-barbarians] are undifferentiated by rank.

The concept of knights as game pieces (as opposed to Development Cards, as per the base game) is not new to [_Traders & Barbarians_][traders-barbarians]: a number of _Catan-News_ scenarios also introduced knight pieces and were later adapted for use with [_Cities & Knights_][cities-knights].

## Gameplay
Refer to the [Game Concepts page][knight-ba] for general rules regarding using knight pieces in "Barbarian Attack".

## Manifest
6 knights are provided for the red, blue, orange, and white players in [_Traders & Barbarians_][traders-barbarians]. These are provided for the green and purple (brown prior to the English 6th edition / 4th international edition) players in the [_Traders & Barbarians_][traders-barbarians] extension.
