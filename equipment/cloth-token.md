**Cloth Tokens** are a piece of equipment used in the [_Seafarers_][seafarers]. They were introduced in the English 6th edition for the scenario "Cloth for Catan", as part of the effort to fully separate the roles of the [Catan chit][catan-chit] from a general-purpose counter to their dedicated role of being a victory point marker.

The introduction of dedicated tokens for cloth is not new, however, to the 4th international edition. In particular, _De Diamanten_ used diamond pieces in much the same role.

## Appearance
Cloth tokens are square tokens that depict cloth (as seen in [_Cities & Knights_][cities-knights]) on one side and half of a victory point marker on the other.

## Gameplay
- Every pair of Cloth Tokens is worth 1 victory point. An unpaired Cloth Token is worth no victory points.

## Manifest
50 Cloth tokens are included with the English 6th edition of [_Seafarers_][seafarers], and 20 additional tokens are included in the [_Seafarers_] extension.
