title: Color Die

In international editions of [_Traders & Barbarians_][traders-barbarians], the **Color Die** is used in the scenario "Barbarian Attack". The color die has never been included in English [_Traders & Barbarians_][traders-barbarians] sets, its function being delegated to one of the regular dice.
