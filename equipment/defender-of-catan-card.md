The **Defender of Catan Card** is a card awarded in earlier editions of [_Cities & Knights_][cities-knights] to players. these cards were removed from the English 6th edition (4th international edition) in favor of victory point markers.

## Gameplay
Defender of Catan cards are rewarded as a result of a Barbarian Attack, if a single player contributes the largest strength contribution to the defense of Catan.

- The supply of Defender of Catan cards are considered infinite, despite the finite supply of cards.

## Manifest
6 Defender of Catan cards were included with [_Cities & Knights_][cities-knights] up to the English 5th edition (3rd international edition).
