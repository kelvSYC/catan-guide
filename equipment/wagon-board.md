The **Wagon Board** is a board used in the scenario "Traders & Barbarians", and outlines the abilities of the Wagon therein. These replace the [Baggage Train][baggage-train] cards introduced in earlier editions of [_Traders & Barbarians_][traders-barbarians], which served the same purpose.

## Appearance
The Wagon Board is a board in the shape of a wagon, with five spaces where a marker can be placed. Each space is associated with a level, which contains information on the Wagon's movement range, ability to drive off Barbarians, and the gold received when [Commodity Tokens][commodity-token] are delivered.

## Gameplay
- At the start of the game, the marker is placed on the first (leftmost) space on the Wagon Board.
- A player may pay the upgrade cost of the wagon, depicted between the spaces on the Wagon Board, to move their marker to the next space on the Wagon Board.
- **Full Upgrade Bonus**: A player with their marker on the final (rightmost) space on the Wagon Board earns one additional victory point.

| Level | Movement | Delivery | Drive Off | Upgrade Cost            |
|-------|----------|----------|-----------|-------------------------|
| 1     | 4        | 1 gold   |           |                         |
| 2     | 5        | 2 gold   | 6         | 1 lumber, 1 ore, 1 wool |
| 3     | 6        | 3 gold   | 5-6       | 1 lumber, 1 ore, 1 wool |
| 4     | 7        | 4 gold   | 4-6       | 2 lumber, 1 ore, 1 wool |
| 5     | 7        | 5 gold   | 3-6       | 2 lumber, 1 ore, 1 wool |

## Manifest
4 Wagon Boards and markers are included with the English 6th edition (4th international edition) of [_Traders & Barbarians_][traders-barbarians]. 2 additional Wagon Boards and markers are included with the English 6th edition (4th international edition) of the [_Traders & Barbarians_][traders-barbarians] extension.
