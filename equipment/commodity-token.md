In the [_Traders & Barbarians_][traders-barbarians] scenario "Traders & Barbarians", **Commodity Tokens** represent the goods that are transported by wagons between the Commodity Hexes.

## Appearance
Each Commodity Token depicts one of the Commodity Hexes on one side, and a good to be transported on the other.

- The Castle Hex supplies tools for the Quarry and sand for the Glassworks, and takes in Marble from the Quarry and Glass from the Glassworks.
- The Glassworks Hex supplies tools for the Quarry and glass for the Castle, and takes in sand from either the Castle or Quarry.
- The Quarry Hex supplies sand for the Glassworks and marble for the Castle, and takes in tools from either the Castle or Glassworks.

## Gameplay
- At the start of the game, the Commodity Tokens are sorted by hex and placed in separate supply piles.
- Whenever a wagon is empty and passes through the center plaza intersection of a Commodity Hex, a token is drawn from the corresponding supply. This determines the destination the wagon will next travel.
  - If there are multiple Commodity Hexes that will take delivery of the good, then the wagon may travel to any of them.
- Once a wagon arrives at the Commodity Hex associated with the good being transported, the wagon is emptied and reloaded with a new Commodity Token.
- Each Commodity Token that has been delivered is worth one victory point.

## Manifest
36 Commodity Tokens are included with [_Traders & Barbarians_][traders-barbarians]:
- 6 Castle/Sand tokens
- 6 Castle/Tools tokens
- 6 Quarry/Marble tokens
- 6 Quarry/Sand tokens
- 6 Glassworks/Glass tokens
- 6 Glassworks/Tools tokens

12 Commodity Tokens are included with the [_Traders & Barbarians_][traders-barbarians] extension:
- 2 Castle/Sand tokens
- 2 Castle/Tools tokens
- 2 Quarry/Marble tokens
- 2 Quarry/Sand tokens
- 2 Glassworks/Glass tokens
- 2 Glassworks/Tools tokens
