Welcome to the _Catan Scenario and Variant Guide_ Live Edition, the official continuation of the _Catan Scenario and Variant Guide_, last seen in 2017.

This site is currently a work in progress, as much of the content is being rewritten for this new format.

## The Base Game

- The First Island - Everything you need to know about the base game, and all its forms
- The Second Islands - Attempts to expand on the base game, predating the expansions

## The Current Expansions

- _Seafarers_
- _Cities & Knights_
- _Traders & Barbarians_
- _Explorers & Pirates_

- _Treasures, Dragons & Adventurers_ - A bonus scenario pack, expanding _Seafarers_ and _Cities & Knights_.
- _Legend of the Sea Robbers_ - An expansion celebrating the 20th anniversary of _Seafarers_.
- _Legend of the Conquerors_ - An expansion celebrating the 20th anniversary of _Cities & Knights_.

## Past Expansions

- _Historische Szenarien_ - A collection of scenarios on a fixed board inspired by history.
- _Das Buch zum Spielen_ - A book of scenarios and variants, some created by fans of the game.
- _Atlantis_ - A select collection of scenarios taken from _Das Buch zum Spielen_, celebrating the 10th anniversary of the base game.
