***Atlantis: Szenarien & Varianten zu Die Siedler von Catan*** is a collection of 8 scenarios and 2 variants, mostly consisting of reprints from [_Das Buch zum Spielen_]({% link products/das-buch-zum-spielen.md %}) but also consisting of original content as well. It was originally released in 2005, as part of the 10th anniversary celebration of the base game. This product was never released in English.

## Scenarios and Variants
- "Atlantis" by Stefan Röse - Note that this was presented as a scenario here, but a variant in [_Das Buch zum Spielen_]({% link products/das-buch-zum-spielen.md %}).
- "Catan Event Cards" by Klaus Teuber - This would later be a standalone English release, before being part of [_Traders & Barbarians_]({% link products/traders-barbarians.md %})
- "In the Jungle" by Klaus Teuber
- "Volcanoes on Catan" by Bastian Schulz
- "The Specialists" by Stefan Risthaus
- "The City Walls" by Klaus Teuber
- "Castles on Catan" by Fritz Gruber
- "The Great River" by Klaus Teuber - This would later be a standalone English release, and would be revised into the [_Traders & Barbarians_]({% link products/traders-barbarians.md %}) scenario "Rivers of Catan"
- "The Harbormaster" by Klaus Teuber - This would be a free download in English, before being included as part of [_Traders & Barbarians_]({% link products/traders-barbarians.md %}), now known as "Harbors of Catan"
- "The Magic 7", based on an idea by Silke Dennenmoser
