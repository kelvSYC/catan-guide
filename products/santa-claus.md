***Catan Scenarios: Santa Claus*** is a mini-expansion distributed for the 2015 holiday season. In Germany, it was part of a board game advent calendar containing 24 mini-expansions from 21 different publishers; this mini-expansion was the first of the 24 to be unlocked.

This mini-expansion has been re-themed a number of times. In 2020, as part of an initiative to promote board gaming amongst the various Asmodee brands (of which _Catan_ is one), an Easter-themed version, titled _Catan Scenarios: Easter Bunny_ was released free as a print-and-play expansion.

## Scenarios
- "Santa Claus"

## Parts List

  - 1 replacement themed Desert
- 11 Gift Tokens - though generic for gameplay purposes, each token shows a different _Catan_ product on one side.
