***Catan Scenarios: Helpers***, originally titled _The Helpers of Catan_, was originally introduced in the 2010 issue of _Catan-News_, the final edition to feature a new scenario or variant. (_Catan-News_ would continue to exist for some time without offering new scenarios or variants, though it would eventually become defunct.)

Although originally presented as a print-and-play scenario, this scenario would be published as a paid product, and localized in English as the first entry in the _Catan Scenarios_ line of scenarios.

The enduring popularity of this mini-expansion has led to similar concepts being included in the core mechanics of other scenarios, as well as a second revision of the scenario released in 2022.

## Scenarios
- "Helpers"

## Equipment
- 12 Helper cards (the first edition only included 10 Helper cards)

**Notes**:
- For the current edition, there is a promotional 13th Helper card.
