***Catan Core + China Map*** is a 2019 Big Box release of the [_Catan_ base game]({% link products/base-game.md %}) alongside an exclusive _Catan Geographies_ map of China. This is akin to earlier Big Box releases where the base game was combined with _Catan Geographies: Germany_, where components can be shared between the two games. This was published exclusively in Chinese for the Hong Kong and Taiwan market, by Chinese language partner [Broadway Games](http://www.broadwaygames.com.hk).

## Scenarios
- "The First Island" - the retronym used to represent the base game
- "China"

## Parts List
In addition to the base game parts, the following additional equipment is included:

- Miscellaneous Player Assets
  - 1 Emperor card per player
  - 6 Great Wall cards per player
- 1 Master Builder scoring bonus
- 7 Monument figures and monument cards, depicting the following monuments:
  - Leshan Giant Buddha (Leshan, Sichuan)
  - Mogao Caves (Dunhuang, Gansu)
  - Oriental Pearl Tower (Pudong New District, Shanghai)
  - Saint Sophia Cathedral (Harbin, Heilongjiang)
  - Statue of Five Goats (Guangzhou, Guangdong)
  - Temple of Heaven (Dongcheng District, Beijing)
  - Terracotta Army (Xi'an, Shaanxi)
