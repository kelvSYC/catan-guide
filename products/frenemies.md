***Catan Scenarios: Frenemies*** is the first scenario credited to Benjamin Teuber, released in 2012. Uniquely, this scenario specifically supports 6-player play, but requires two copies of this product.

Although this product is available as separate English and international sets, aside from the rule sheet, all components are multilingual: where there is a difference between the English and international art assets, the English version is printed on one side while the international version is printed on the other.

## Scenarios
- "Frenemies"

## Parts List
- 1 Guild Hall board (English art one side, international art the other side)
- 8 Catan chits
- Favor Tokens
  - 8 Trader Guild
  - 8 Merchant Guild
  - 8 Road Builder Guild
  - 17 Scholar Guild
  - 17 Master Builder Guild
