***Die Siedler von Catan: Historische Szenarien I - Alexander der Große & Cheops*** is a 1998 expansion to _Catan_. The first of the two "historical scenarios" expansions, this expansion takes the gameplay mechanics of _Catan_ and applies it to a historical context.

This expansion was first previewed in the 2/1997 issue of _Siedler-Zeitung_, with the sneak peak of a scenario introduced as "Rameses".

No English version was ever released, though German versions were available for release with a bundled English translation.

## Scenarios
- "Alexander the Great"
- "Cheops"
  - "Uprising in Egypt"

## Parts List
- 1 Double-sided game board
- Player Pieces
  - "Cheops"
    - 11 Pyramid blocks per player
    - 8 cardboard ships per player
- 40 gold pieces
- Miscellaneous Scenario-Specific Equipment
  - "Alexander the Great"
    - 28 Event tokens
    - 1 Alexander figure
  - "Cheops"
    - 16 neutral Pyramid blocks
    - 1 Pharaoh's Blessing scoring bonus
  - 1 First Advisor scoring bonus for "Alexander the Great" / Pharaoh's Curse scoring bonus for "Cheops"
  - 1 Second Advisor scoring bonus for "Alexander the Great" / Pharaoh's Curse scoring bonus for "Cheops"
  - 1 Third Advisor scoring bonus for "Alexander the Great" / Pharaoh's Curse scoring bonus for "Cheops"
