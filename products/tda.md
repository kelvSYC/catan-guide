***Treasures, Dragons & Adventurers*** is an expansion of _Catan_, originally released in 2009 as an appreciation to longtime fans, though it would be in 2019 when it was released in English. As a fan appreciation expansion, it was originally an exclusive to the Catan Shop, sold as a parts sheet an informal rulebook without a box. In 2017, it was redesigned as a formal boxed product, with brown used as part of the color-coding scheme.

The scenarios of this expansion consist of a number of scenarios introduced in early _Catan_ video games, primarily the early 2000s game _Catan: Cities & Knights_. This expansion also reintroduces the fan-favorite scenario "Greater Catan" after it was removed from [_Seafarers_]({% link products/seafarers.md %}) in the English 4th edition and 2nd international editions. A re-themed version of a long-standing free-to-download scenario in "The Desert Dragons" was also reintroduced. All in all, only one scenario, "Into the Unknown", is completely new in this expansion, and even then, the 3-player version was released separately, for free, in the 2009 edition of _Catan-News_, to promote this expansion.

As a fan appreciation expansion, this expansion's scenarios require [_Seafarers_]({% link products/seafarers.md %}), with many scenarios requiring [_Cities & Knights_]({% link products/cities-knights.md %}). Although this expansion is strictly for four players without an extension, it is to be noted that a number of historical setups of some of the scenarios had six-player setups. Similarly, some historical setups of some of the scenarios did not require [_Cities & Knights_]({% link products/cities-knights.md %}), where they are required for the scenarios as presented in this expansion.

## Scenarios
- "The Treasure Islands"
- "Into the Unknown"
- "Greater Catan"
- "The Desert Dragons"
- "The Great Canal"
- "Enchanted Land"
