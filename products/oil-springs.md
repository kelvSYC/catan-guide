***Catan Scenarios: Oil Springs*** is a scenario created in 2011 by Erik Assadourian and Ty Hansen, in cooperation with [Transforming Cultures Project of the Worldwatch Institute](http://www.transformingcultures.org), to raise awareness of the effects that the use of oil has on the environment. It can be obtained for free from [its official website](http://www.oilsprings.catan.com), but pre-printed copies are also available.

The graphics of this expansion is designed to be seamless with the English 4th edition of the games.

## Scenarios
- "Oil Springs"
