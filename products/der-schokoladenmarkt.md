***Die Siedler von Catan: Der Schokoladenmarkt*** is a 2010 mini-expansion for _Catan_, in collaboration with German chocolate company [Ritter Sport](http://www.ritter-sport.com/en). Although Ritter Sport produces chocolates for the English speaking market, this mini-expansion was never released in English.

## Scenarios
- "The Chocolate Market"
