***Die Siedler von Catan: Historische Szenarien II - Troja & Die Große Mauer*** is the second of the two historical scenario expansions to _Catan_. Like its predecessor, it contains two scenarios taken from historical contexts, making it a spiritual predecessor of the _Catan Histories_ line of standalone games. Unlike the previous expansion, however, both of the scenarios support six-player play with the base game extension. The game board is printed on three panels, and a four-player game is played using only two of the panels.

As with its predecessor, no English version was ever published, though German copies were available for sale, bundled with an English rules translation.

## Scenarios
- "Troy"
- "The Great Wall"
