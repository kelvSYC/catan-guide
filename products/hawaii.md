***Catan: Seafarers Scenario: Hawaii*** was originally released as the 2017 entry in the _Catan Geographies_ series of scenarios. Uniquely among the scenarios of that line, it requires [_Seafarers_]({% link products/seafarers.md %}) to play.

The original release of the scenario was being part of the _Catan Geographies_ series, and was only for the English version. Subsequent releases, which saw both an English and international edition release, made this a standalone scenario, released alongside [_Legend of the Sea Robbers_]({% link products/sea-robbers.md %}). The standalone scenario adds a tile sheet for additional equipment not present in either the base game or [_Seafarers_]({% link products/seafarers.md %}).

## Scenarios
- Hawaii

## Parts List
The original release only includes the game board; all other components are available in the re-release.

- 1 Game board
- Player Pieces
  - 1 Fishing Boat marker per player
- Miscellaneous Player Equipment
  - 1 Fish Market reference card per player
- Fish tokens
  - 13 - 1 Fish
  - 6 - 2 Fish
  - 6 - 3 Fish
