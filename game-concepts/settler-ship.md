A **Settler Ship** is a ship that is loaded with a Settler piece. They are the primary means of expanding beyond the starting island in [_Explorers & Pirates_][explorers-pirates].

The terminology was originally introduced with the initial release of [_Explorers & Pirates_][explorers-pirates] as part of the English 4th edition, but was removed in the English 6th edition.

## Gameplay
- Settler ships do not confer any additional movement or other privileges compared to empty ships or ships loaded with other cargo.
- **Settlement**: If a Settler Ship is located on a path adjacent to land, a player may build a Settlement on either of the intersections on either end of the path. To do so, both the Settler and Ship pieces are returned to the player's supply.
  - A player must still abide by the Distance Rule when placing the Settlement.
