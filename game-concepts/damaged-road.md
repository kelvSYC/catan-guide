When playing with [Event Cards][event-cards], a Road may become damaged from an event. This creates a **Damaged Road**.

## Gameplay
- Damaged Roads are created when the Earthquake event occurs. To denote that a Road is damaged, it is turned sideways, perpendicular to the path it lies on.
- A player may not build additional Roads until all of their Damaged Roads are repaired.
- Players may not build buildings on intersections on either end of their own Damaged Roads.
- A Damaged Road still contributes to determining the owner of the Longest Route scoring bonus.
- The "Road Building" Development Card may be used to repair Damaged Roads.

{% include expansion-box-open.html layout="cities-knights" %}
The following additional rules are in effect when playing with [_Cities & Knights_][cities-knights]:
- The ["Diplomacy" Progress Card][politics-progress-card] cannot be used to repair or remove a Damaged Road.
- The ["Road Building" Progress Card][science-progress-card] may be used to repair a Damaged Road.
