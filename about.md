The _Catan Scenario and Variant Guide_ is a guide to all officially released scenarios and variants for _Catan_, a board game created by Klaus Teuber in 1995. The game has grown over the 30+ years into a modern classic and a gateway game into the world of board gaming.

The _Guide_ originally started as a PDF document, but largely became unmanageable due to spanning over 450 pages when last released in 2017. For this Live version, the _Guide_ has been completely rewritten to make it easier to maintain.

In addition to a rules summary of each scenario and variant, if the scenario is not available in English, then a best effort is made to replicate the scenario using only components available in English.
